Instructions

  • In this experiment you will be asked to rate the similarity of certain words using a slider bar.
  • One end of the slider will represent complete dissimilarity while the other end will represent complete similarity.
  • On some trials, you will also be asked to guess how other people would respond.
  • On those trials, you will see a section of the slider bar has been highlighted and will be asked to guess how many people out of a 100 would place their sliders inside that range.
  • Please think carefully about each question.
  • There are 270 trials in total and the task is expected to take you about 20 minutes.
